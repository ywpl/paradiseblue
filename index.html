About the boat

Paradise Blue is not just a boat — it’s a lifestyle. Proudly designed and handcrafted in Bayswater, Western Australia, Paradise Blue introduces a revolutionary style of pontoon boat engineered for comfort, sustainability, and unforgettable adventures.

Whether you're cruising the coast, relaxing on the river, or entertaining friends on the water, Paradise Blue delivers a perfect blend of innovation and leisure. With sleek lines, modern finishes, and eco-conscious design, this boat is built to turn heads and create memories.

We use solar regenerated batteries to power your journey responsibly. Our boat comes with a fully enclosed shower and toilet, offering convenience and privacy on the go. There's comfortable lounge seating for 8 people, perfect for socializing and relaxing. The full coverage roof ensures protection from the sun and the elements. Plus, an 80L built-in fridge keeps your refreshments cool all day long.

Paradise Blue is perfect for families, friends, and explorers who want more from their time on the water — with style, substance, and sustainability at its core.

From concept to construction, every detail is carefully crafted by skilled hands right here in WA — combining modern design with practical Australian know-how. It’s a boat made for our waters, our climate, and our way of life.
